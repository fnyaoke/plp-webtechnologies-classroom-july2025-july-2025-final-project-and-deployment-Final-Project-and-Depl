Key Projects & Initiatives

Wawa Aba Womanist Leadership Institute

A Pan-African initiative training women leaders in diversity, theology, and justice-centered leadership. Organized conferences, mentorship, and regional engagement across Africa and the diaspora.

Cosmopolitan Affirming Community

Faith-rooted work that builds inclusive spiritual spaces for LGBTQI+ persons, providing advocacy, healing, and solidarity in the face of exclusion and discrimination.

Interconnected Justice

A Pan-African movement planning the Desmond Tutu Conference to examine colonization, dominionism, and supremacy culture, fostering solidarity and liberation for people of color.
