About Me

Hello! My name is Florence Nyaoke. I am a software developer, womanist leader, writer, and advocate committed to advancing justice, equity, and dignity for marginalized communities. My work spans across faith, feminist, and Pan-Africanist movements, where I create spaces for healing, leadership, and inclusive community building.

I bring experience in communications, leadership development, and movement building, including spearheading initiatives like the Wawa Aba Womanist Leadership Institute, the We Are Kenyans Too Campaign, and advocacy within the Cosmopolitan Affirming Community. My passion lies in combining storytelling, strategy, and solidarity to push for systemic change.

Beyond activism, I write reflections blending African proverbs, womanist theology, and indigenous spirituality to inspire leadership rooted in wholeness and justice.
